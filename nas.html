Designed for Convenience

A Networked Attached Storage, or NAS, server enables the user to access files from any computer, from anywhere the internet can be accessed. If you choose to put your digitalized movie files on such a device, you could access them without the clutter of usb drives or the loss of valuable space on your daily computer.
